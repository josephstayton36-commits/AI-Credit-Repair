Money & Membership Questions

We’d rather you understand exactly what you’re saying yes to.

Is there a contract?

No long-term contracts. You can cancel future renewals any time. Because credit repair results depend on your situation and actions, past results don’t guarantee a specific outcome.

Do you guarantee a specific score increase?

No. That would be dishonest. We can’t control the bureaus or creditors. We focus on strategy, accuracy, and helping you make stronger decisions.

Can I upgrade or downgrade later?

Yes. You can move between plans as your goals and budget change.

Is this legal and compliant?

We use AI as a tool to help you understand your options and organize your strategy. We do not provide legal, tax, or investment advice. You should always verify information and consult qualified professionals for those areas.
